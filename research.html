Research Areas

Our lab focuses on advancing privacy-preserving and accessible technologies in emerging interactive systems. We study how people perceive, trust, and interact with Generative AI, alongside privacy and security challenges in AR/VR environments.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Trust, Privacy and Accessibility Perception of GenAI among Blind and Low Vision People

As Generative AI technologies become embedded in everyday life, issues of trust, privacy, accessibility, and transparency become especially critical for blind and low-vision users.

Privacy, Trust, and Accessibility in Assistive Augmented Reality

This research investigates privacy, trust, and accessibility challenges in assistive augmented reality systems, aiming to support inclusive and trustworthy design.

Collaborative Technology

Privacy Barriers for BLV Users Using Instant Messaging Applications

This project explores privacy challenges that blind and low-vision users face when using messaging applications.

Accessibility of Collaborative Technologies for BLV Professionals

Real-time collaboration tools are essential yet often inaccessible for BLV professionals.
